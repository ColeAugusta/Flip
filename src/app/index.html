About Flip

Vision:

Flip is designed to provide an easy-to-use hand movement tracking software for reading eBooks or PDFs on devices equipped with a camera. While other hand detection software can be unreliable and frustrating, Flip aims to offer a solution that users can consistently trust. With Flip, users can "swipe" their hands in front of a connected camera, prompting the device to navigate accordingly. We offer a free, open-source Chrome extension for public use. Our project leverages Python with OpenCV and NumPy for hand movement tracking, and potentially JavaScript/PyScript for Chrome extension development.

Semester Goals:

  • Research and familiarize ourselves with Python's OpenCV and NumPy libraries.
  • Accurately track user hand movements, at minimum for basic gestures like swiping left or right.
  • Enable users to seamlessly flip through eBooks/PDFs on their devices.
  • Package our movement tracker capabilities into a Chrome Extension for easy access.
